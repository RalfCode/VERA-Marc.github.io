About us:

We were once a neat student association, and that's where the name comes from: Veri Et Recti Amici (True And Sincere Friends). Today it is a fully-fledged concert stage. Many bands, which later became world famous, have once played here, such as: U2, Joy Division, Simple Minds, Nick Cave, Henry Rollins, Sonic Youth, Soundgarden, Slayer, Nirvana, Pearl Jam, Weezer, The White Stripes, At The Drive-in, Mogwai, Franz Ferdinand, Editors, Interpol, Elbow, The Hives, Triggerfinger, Nothing But Thieves, Dua Lipa and many others.

Activities

Every week we organize two to three, and sometimes four concerts and every Tuesday there is Vera Zienema. In addition, there is also a Downstage concert and a dance evening every Saturday.

Volunteers

All our activities are made possible by more than 200 volunteer employees. They hold positions in many subgroups, such as: bar, light, technology, art division, zieema and many more.
